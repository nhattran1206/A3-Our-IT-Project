Group reflection





Overall



As a whole group, we think that the project development during these 4 weeks went well overall, even though we had also encountered some challenges during the time. Firstly, it was a great challenge that one of our team members could not join us during our weekly meeting due to his sickness. An felt very regretful for not being able to take part in the offline meeting, but the team understood the situation, sympathized with him, and gave him a lot of support during his tough time. But, thanks to the social network, we were able to communicate with each other easily, even though one of our members got caught with Covid. Furthermore, another major drawback that our team has was the lack of preparation for the presentation. For starters, we did not spend enough time creating a proper presentation slide, so the slide was not as polished as we expected.


After going through these experiences, our team has learned a lot of different lessons from these mistakes. The first lesson that we can learn from An’s case is that we should have responsibilities for our own health because if you have a problem, it will also affect the production rate of the group. Secondly, we must pay more attention to our time management. It is essential to finish work on schedule so that the project can be completed on time.


As a team, after this project we promise that we will learn how to be more proactive and focusing on working as a team more than individually.
